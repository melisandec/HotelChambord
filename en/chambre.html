A Journey into Royal Elegance

The rooms at Hotel Chambord embody the soul of grand French châteaux, offering a perfect blend of historical refinement and modern comfort. Each category – from the charming Classic Rooms to the sumptuous Suites – is designed to immerse our guests in a world of majesty and elegance.

With floral wallpapers, canopy beds draped in luxurious fabrics, waxed parquet floors, and period furniture, every detail evokes the splendor of royal residences. No matter which type of room you choose, you will be transported to an authentic and enchanting setting where comfort meets a decor worthy of the finest pages of French history.

Classic Room

Our Classic Rooms embody the essence of French charm, ideal for an intimate stay in a refined setting. The walls are adorned with floral wallpapers reminiscent of the grand châteaux salons, while the period furniture, consisting of delicately carved wooden desks and tufted armchairs, invites serenity. The waxed parquet reflects the soft light of a crystal chandelier, and the canopy bed draped in noble fabrics ensures absolute comfort. These rooms offer a royal experience, perfect for immersing oneself in the timeless ambiance of Château de Chambord.

Comfort Room

The Comfort Rooms offer a warm and elegant ambiance, ideal for travelers seeking sophistication. Their refined decor blends soft colors and floral patterns inherited from the most beautiful French châteaux. The canopy beds draped in luxurious curtains invite a night worthy of nobility, while the wooden desks and brass lamps add a touch of authenticity. Marble touches in the bathroom and views of elegant Paris complete the atmosphere of discreet refinement.

Deluxe Room

Our Deluxe Rooms are a true homage to the royal art of living. Every detail has been designed to offer an unforgettable experience: imposing chandeliers, floral tapestries, and marble statues that evoke the opulence of the grand reception halls of the great châteaux. The canopy bed, adorned with sumptuous draperies, is an invitation to princely rest. The waxed parquet and carved wooden furniture, combined with a luxurious bathroom, make every moment spent in these rooms exceptional. Spacious and bathed in light, they embody French luxury.

Our Suites

Our Suites, true jewels of the Hotel Chambord, offer a complete immersion into the royal universe of the Château de Chambord. These sumptuous spaces, decorated with unparalleled care, blend floral wall frescoes, antique furniture, and architectural details inspired by the grand salons of the Renaissance. The private lounge is adorned with solid wood furniture, marble statues, and embroidered curtains. The master bedroom, dominated by a majestic canopy bed, is a sanctuary of luxury. The marble bathrooms, equipped with deep bathtubs and sculpted basins, add an extra touch of splendor. The Suites are designed for guests who wish to experience royal living in the heart of Paris.
