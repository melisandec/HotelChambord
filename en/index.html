History & Heritage

The history of Hotel Chambord is closely linked to that of the majestic Château de Chambord, a jewel of French Renaissance architecture. Inspired by the grandeur and elegance of this historic monument, Hotel Chambord opened its doors just a month ago, bringing a fresh breath to the heart of Paris.

Although recent, this iconic luxury establishment embodies the spirit of the château in a contemporary setting, skillfully blending history and modernity. Its sumptuous interiors and refined architecture have already made it a true gem of the French capital, attracting the attention of personalities from the world of politics, culture, and business.

Meanwhile, the Château de Chambord Hotel, located near the historic estate, offers visitors a unique experience, allowing them to stay close to this architectural masterpiece. Both establishments, each in their own way, perpetuate the heritage and prestige of the Château de Chambord, offering guests an immersion in French history and luxury.

Castle Room

Hotel Chambord offers a selection of elegant rooms and suites, providing exceptional comfort and breathtaking views of the City of Light. Each room is carefully decorated in a contemporary style, combining refinement and modernity to create a warm and welcoming living space.

The spacious and bright suites feature large windows offering panoramic views of Paris, while the classic and comfortable rooms are ideal for a solo stay or for two.

All rooms and suites are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. Some suites also have a separate living area and a spa bath for an even more luxurious stay.

Gastronomic Experiences

Hotel Chambord offers an exceptional culinary experience with its Michelin-starred restaurant and terrace brasserie. The gourmet restaurant, led by Michelin-starred chef Jean-Pierre Lefevre, offers refined and creative cuisine, highlighting seasonal products and French flavors.

The brasserie, located on the hotel's Greco-Roman terrace, provides a relaxed and friendly atmosphere, perfect for an outdoor lunch or a romantic dinner. You can enjoy traditional dishes with a twist, refreshing cocktails, and a selection of fine wines.

Whether you are a gourmet enthusiast or simply looking for a unique culinary experience, our restaurant and brasserie will satisfy all your desires. Come and discover delicious dishes and unforgettable moments in an exceptional setting.

Spa & Wellness

The spa at Hotel Chambord is a true haven of peace, offering a comprehensive range of relaxing and revitalizing treatments. Our team of experienced therapists welcomes you in an elegant and soothing setting, where you can recharge and relax in complete serenity.

Discover our exclusive treatment menu, including relaxing massages, hydrating facials, nourishing body wraps, and complete wellness rituals. Our spa is equipped with a sauna, hammam, jacuzzi, and indoor pool for an optimal relaxation experience.

Whether you want to treat yourself to a moment of relaxation or benefit from therapeutic treatments, our spa will meet all your expectations. Come and enjoy a break of well-being and serenity in a luxurious and refined setting.
